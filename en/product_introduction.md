# Product Introduction

## Product Overview

**DataEye Insight** is an end-to-end data intelligence and growth optimization platform that covers **data ingestion, cleansing, modeling, analytics, and decision optimization**.

By combining deep industry expertise with large-scale data assets, the platform provides industry-ready dashboards for user acquisition and monetization, predictive business models, and self-service analytics tools.
Our mission is to help enterprises transform data into insight and insight into sustained business growth.

## Product Positioning

**DataEye Insight = Data Analytics Platform + Growth Modeling Engine + Industry-Ready Solutions**

The platform supports both **SaaS and on-premise deployment**, enabling unified ingestion and integration of multi-source data (UA, monetization, user behavior, attribution, etc.).

Through standardized data modeling and AI-powered analytics, DataEye Insight enables enterprises to:

- Monitor UA and monetization data in real time
- Conduct multidimensional cross-analysis
- Leverage AI forecasting and budget allocation models
- Perform flexible, self-service visualization and data exploration

Ultimately empowering teams to make **precise, data-driven decisions** and achieve **maximum business value.**

## Key Advantages & Core Capabilities

### IAA / IAP / Hybrid Monetization Modeling

For major business models such as **IAA, IAP, and IAA+IAP**, the platform provides **six standardized industry models and dashboards**, featuring:

- Plug-and-play usage with no repetitive modeling
- Deep visibility into user lifecycle value
- Faster identification of growth levers and bottlenecks
- Support for fully refined lifecycle operations

### Comprehensive Analytical Models & Tools

DataEye Insight includes a full set of powerful yet user-friendly analytics models:

- **Event Analysis** – trends, parameters, behavior paths
- **Retention Analysis** – cohort retention, channel retention, multiple retention logics
- **Funnel Analysis** – conversion flows and drop-off diagnosis
- **Cross-Analysis** – multi-dimension, multi-metric slicing and drill-down
- **Dual-Engine Self-Service Analytics**
  - SQL Views for analysts
  - Drag-and-drop visual analysis for non-technical users

With rich visualization options and flexible customization, teams can analyze faster, deeper, and more independently.

### AI Forecasting Models

Powered by industry-specific datasets and advanced machine learning algorithms, the platform accurately forecasts:

- ROI (Return on Investment)
- ARPU / ARPPU
- LTV (Lifetime Value)

These models provide forward-looking insights to help operators make **timely, evidence-based optimizations**.

### AI Budget Allocation

Our AI-driven budget allocation engine optimizes daily ad spend distribution based on:

- The last 7 days of historical campaign data
- Daily available budget
- Target ROI

The model automatically determines the optimal budget for each ad account / campaign to **maximize overall ROI**, reducing manual workload and improving efficiency.

### Flexible Data Ingestion

DataEye Insight supports multiple ingestion methods to fit different enterprise environments:

- **Client-side SDK**
- **Server-side SDK**
- **API Import**
- **Third-party platform integrations**

Combined with built-in cleansing and modeling, the platform ensures unified, high-quality data for analysis and prediction.
